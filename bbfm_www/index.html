Why use our full Obyte merchant solution?

With our merchant solution your website will show a quick Obyte payment button similar to the well known Paypal button. It is a pure script implementation: nothing to install.

In a minute you will be able to accept Obyte payments from mobile or desktop customers for any good or service you sell on the internet at a minimal cost and effort.

Here is how it works:

  • Obyte quick payment button embeds your unique order id, your Obyte merchant address and the amount to be paid by your customer.
  • A backlink to our server from your checkout page and the quick payment button is displayed (refer to the "example.html" page on Github).
  • A click or a QR scan on the customer end and his Obyte wallet opens with the amount to be paid.
  • Once the payment is confirmed by the network, we notify you (by a GET, a POST to your server or an email or even both, upon your choice) that we received the Bytes so that your can process your customer order on your end.
  • We immediatly send you the payment minus our commission (1000 Bytes + 0.9% fee).
  • That's all!
